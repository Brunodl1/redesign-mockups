## Design tokens (from raw HTML — weak old theme, so elevate)
- **Fonts:** theme ships **Helvetica** (default). Free to elevate → a rugged outdoor system: a bold
  condensed/grotesque **display** (e.g. **Oswald** or **Archivo**/**Anton** for headings) + clean sans
  **body** (Inter/Archivo). Match a "heritage outdoor brand" feel.
- **Colors:** old theme exposes little (#363636, #000, #fff). Silverfoot's identity is outdoor/mountain
  + Canadiana (the Pacific Otter Red collar = red). Build a rugged palette: deep forest/pine green +
  warm cream/canvas + charcoal ink + a **Canada-red** accent (echoes the product + made-in-Canada),
  optional tan/leather. (Confirm/anchor red from the real product photo.)
