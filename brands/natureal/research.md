# Natureal — research

**URL:** https://natureal.co/ · **Page:** home · **Language:** EN · **Niche:** skincare-cosmetics (organic personal care / beauty)

## Brand
- Natureal — **single-brand organic personal-care / beauty** ("Organic Made Affordable"). Hair, skin, body,
  bath: clay masks, organic hair dye, scalp shampoos, body lotions, shower gels, foam hand washes, pimple
  patches. **Botanically sourced from around the world** — Australian tea tree, Brazilian spearmint, Indian
  turmeric, Japanese yuzu/sakura, Madagascar vanilla, Dead Sea mud. **Vegan, no synthetic additives**,
  premium organic ingredients at accessible prices. NOT supplements.
- **ICP:** health-conscious, eco-aware women ~25–45 who want clean/natural ingredient-transparent personal
  care that aligns with their values — without the premium-organic price tag.
- **USP:** premium, vegan, botanically-sourced organic personal care — no synthetic additives — at honest, accessible prices.
- **Pains:** mainstream products full of synthetics they don't trust; organic brands too expensive; hard to find vegan/ethical care that works.
- **Desires:** clean ingredient-transparent products they feel good using; premium organic quality at fair prices; sustainable, vegan, values-aligned.
- **Tone:** clean, approachable, nature-focused, ingredient-proud, accessible (not gatekeeping).
