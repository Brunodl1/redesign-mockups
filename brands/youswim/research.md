# YouSwim — research

**URL:** https://youswim.com/ · **Page:** home · **Language:** EN · **Niche:** swimwear (size-inclusive)

## Brand
- YouSwim — **size-inclusive / "two sizes fit all"** swimwear. Just **Stretch I (US 2–12)** and
  **Stretch II (US 14–24)** in proprietary **Versoform™ four-way-stretch** ribbed fabric that adapts to
  your body. "The world's most comfortable swimsuit." Made in England, ethical/low-volume, FSC packaging,
  15-sec fit quiz, next-day UK shipping + easy returns. Also a small lifestyle range (soaps, balms).
- **ICP:** women (18–45+) of diverse body types tired of sizing anxiety / fit-between-sizes / body changes;
  want one simple system, comfort-first, ethical. Converts on **empathy + proof** (real bodies), not luxury.
- **Hook / lever:** ONE stretchy suit per range fits a huge span — show the SAME fabric on MANY real bodies,
  + the two-tier size selector + fit quiz. "Designed for the body you're in."
- **USP:** two stretchy sizes + Versoform 4-way stretch that adapts to you — no more sizing anxiety.
- **Pains:** sizing charts never match your body; body changes force re-shopping; rigid sizing makes YOU conform.
- **Desires:** one system across your body's range; comfort/support without restriction; ethical & durable.
- **Tone:** empowering, inclusive, body-positive, comfort-obsessed, celebratory of change.
