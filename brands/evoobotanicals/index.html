Good to know

Questions, answered

Is Aura greasy?

No — it's a lightweight oil-serum that absorbs fully, leaving skin balanced and supple, not shiny.

Does it have fragrance?

It's free from synthetic fragrance. The fresh, green, herbal scent comes only from the botanicals (spearmint, lemongrass, green tea, pandan).

When do I use it?

Each morning. Warm 1–2 pumps, press into clean skin, then follow with moisturiser and SPF.

Is it good for sensitive skin?

Yes — waterless, no synthetic fragrance, and calming native botanicals like kawakawa make it well suited to sensitive, barrier-compromised skin.

How is it made?

We slow-infuse botanicals into New Zealand extra virgin olive oil over weeks — never blended at scale — for a nutrient-dense oil-serum.

Is it cruelty-free and natural?

Yes — cruelty-free, waterless and made in Akaroa, New Zealand, with naturally derived botanical ingredients.
