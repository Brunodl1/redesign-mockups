- **URL:** https://strategyskincare.com/  ·  **Brand (logo/og):** "Strategy Skin"
- **Language:** EN (`<html lang=en>`, US/UK English copy, not Québec)
- **Niche:** skincare-cosmetics (clinical-luxe, single-hero-product)
- **Page:** home  ·  **Hero variant:** full-bleed editorial (faithful to original)

## ICP / positioning
Skin-conscious women ~28–55 who want **retinoid results without irritation**.
Sells on *science + restraint + prestige*. Single hero SKU. Buying emotion:
"a smarter, gentler vitamin-A that actually works on sensitive skin." Objection to
answer: *"will a 'gentle' retinal really do anything?"* → answer with the
encapsulation science, reviews, results language.

## Product
- **The A Retinal Serum (Youth Strategy)** — **$195**
- USP: rapid-delivery, **encapsulated retinal** (retinaldehyde) — absorbed faster &
  more effectively than retinol, **far less irritating**, great for sensitive skin.
- Hero line: **"A STRATEGY WITH DEPTH"**
- Subhead: *"The rapid-delivery retinal serum that balances, brightens and visibly
  improves the appearance of lines and wrinkles — without causing irritation."*
- Science: retinal absorbed faster than retinol; encapsulation bypasses the barrier,
  delivers each molecule into the cell; excess stored & slow-released → less irritation.
