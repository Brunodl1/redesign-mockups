Our Story

Escape
the mundane.

Unbound was born from a restless desire to wander — the kind that strikes at 2 a.m. when you catch a thread of salt air through a cracked window and suddenly you're somewhere else entirely. We bottle that feeling. Each candle is hand-poured in small batches in Miami, scented to carry you to a real place you've dreamed of visiting: the cliff-side lemon groves of the Amalfi Coast, the cedar forests of the Pacific Northwest, the night markets of Marrakech at dusk.

No two pours are identical. No two journeys are either. Take a deep breath — you're already there.

You are unbound.
