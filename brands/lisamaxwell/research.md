# Lisa Maxwell — research (home page redesign)

## Brand
- **Lisa Maxwell** (lisamaxwell.ca) — Toronto-based **dainty gemstone/crystal jewelry**, handmade,
  **14K gold-filled**. Signature: delicate beaded bracelets, trillium necklaces, "dainty" stacking
  sets, gemstone earrings — each crystal chosen for an **intention / energy** (balance, insight,
  rebalance, "charmed"). Wellness-meets-luxe, feminine, soulful.
- **Hero products:** Dainty Golden Bracelet, Trinity trio bracelets, Aquamarine Trillium necklace,
  gemstone luxe bracelets (malachite+moonstone, pink opal, strawberry quartz), stacking sets.
- **ICP:** women 25–55 self-purchasing or gifting; drawn to meaningful, pretty, everyday gemstone
  pieces with a crystal-energy/intention story; value handmade, ethically made, gold-filled (not
  plated), gift-ready. Repeat/collector behaviour (stacking).
- **USP / angles:** real gemstones + intentions (energy/balance), 14K gold-filled durability,
  handmade in Toronto, dainty everyday-wearable, giftable sets, build-your-stack.
- **Tone:** warm, soulful, feminine, a touch boho-luxe — but should be elevated (calm & premium),
  not cluttered/craft-fair.

## Language
- **English.** `lang="en"`, `.ca` but **Toronto/Ontario** (not Québec); no FR toggle / hreflang /
  FR copy found. Keep EN. (Same call as Pehr.)

## Design tokens (from raw HTML)
- **Fonts:** theme ships essentially **Arial** (weak default) → free to elevate. Per jewelry
  reference, pair a refined high-contrast **serif display (Cormorant Garamond)** with a light clean
  **sans (Jost)** for UI/body — elegant + soulful, fits gemstone-wellness femininity.
- **Colors (real, from HTML):** deep **emerald-teal `#108474`** (dominant brand color), bright
  **mint `#1fd3c0`**, **periwinkle `#6370a7`**, **navy `#2b3360`/`#313d88`**, **hot-pink `#ff007b`**
  accent; gold comes from product photos. → Anchor on emerald-teal as the deep accent (top
  bar/footer/buttons), soft **ivory/cream** base, warm ink, **gold hairlines from photos**, with
  periwinkle/blush as sparing secondary. Elevated, not loud.
