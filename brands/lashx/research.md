# LAshX — research + tokens + image plan

## Brand
- **Name:** LAshX® (Makeup Mandy, Inc.) — US brand/clinic, beauty.
- **Sells:** lash-extension-SAFE care & makeup system — gel lash cleanser, PRO/Line
  & DRAMA/Line treatment eyeliners (lash-growth/serum), Mega Volume Mascara,
  pink silicone mascara wands, heated lash curler, aftercare/removal kits, gift kits.
- **USP:** "Makeup Design to Improve Lash **Health** and Lash Extension Wear" —
  results-based, lash-safe formulations that make extensions last longer & keep
  natural lashes healthy. Founder = lash tech / clinic (Makeup Mandy Jacobellis).
- **Proof:** E! Entertainment — "THE place to get Red Carpet Ready". Pro/salon
  credibility (used by lash technicians), "Products for Professionals".
- **ICP:** women with lash extensions (and the techs who serve them) who don't want
  their makeup/cleanser to dissolve the glue or damage lashes. Buying emotion:
  "keep my extensions full & lasting + my real lashes healthy, with products that
  are actually lash-safe." Objection: "is 'lash-safe' real or marketing?" → answer
  with the science/ingredients, pro endorsement, reviews.
- **Tone:** professional + aspirational beauty authority; "red carpet ready".
- **Language:** **EN** (US brand). Build in English.
- **Niche:** skincare-cosmetics (beauty). Reference exists.
