Our Story

A 135-year-old orchard,
brought back to life

Two brothers from Michigan fell in love with a neglected 135-year-old olive grove tucked into the hills of California. They spent years restoring it — tree by tree, season by season — learning its rhythms and coaxing it back to life.

Working alongside an Italian master miller, they cold-press in small batches the very same day the fruit is picked. Every bottle is single-estate and fully traceable — made the way real olive oil should be, for the table, and for your health.

— The Nuvo family
