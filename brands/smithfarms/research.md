# Research — Smith Farms (smithfarmsproducts.com)

## What they sell
Clean, plant-based skincare made on a family farm in Western Québec (family since the 1970s, brand since 2009). Face care, body care, lip care, plus refill packs, value/gift sets, and eco supplies. Mid-premium price points.

## ICP
Skin-conscious women 30–60, sensitive / rosacea / dry / mature or acne-prone skin, who want **clean, gentle, results-driven** products and care about **sustainability + supporting a small Canadian family business**. Reviews skew "sensitive skin / rosacea / gentle / lovely scent / minimal packaging."

## USP / proof
- **Clean + gentle:** plant-based, cruelty-free, sensitive-skin friendly (rosacea-safe per reviews).
- **Sustainable:** low waste, refill packs + reusable glass bottles, recyclable kraft packaging.
- **Heritage + trust:** family farm, made in Canada since 2009; produced on-site.
- **Earned press:** Chatelaine, FASHION, Clin d'œil, Reader's Digest, Parent (real logos on current site — load-bearing trust).
- **Real reviews:** strong testimonials (hand cream, sensitive-skin praise).
- Offers: free shipping $60+, Subscribe & Save up to 15%, free 7-day sample kit, rewards program, newsletter 15% off.
