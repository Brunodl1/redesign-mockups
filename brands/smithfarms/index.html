Since 2009

Made by hand on our family farm in Québec.

In our family since the 1970s, our farm in Western Québec is where it all happens — every cream, oil and soap is still made on the grounds, in small batches, by the people whose name is on the bottle.

It’s skincare evocative of a simpler, gentler way of living — and a promise that what touches your skin is something we’d use on our own.
