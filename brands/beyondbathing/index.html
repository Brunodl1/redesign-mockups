Our Story

Made by two best friends who get it

We went looking for swimwear that would actually cover us, hold us in, and keep the sun off — and came up empty every single time. So we stopped searching and started sewing. Beyond Bathing was born in Canada, designed by two of us for the rest of us: real bodies, real summers, zero tugging.

No more skimpy scraps that ride up the second you stand. No more squinting at "one size flatters all." Just thoughtful, sun-smart swim you can wave hello in, splash in, and forget you're even wearing.

— Velvet & Rachel, co-founders
