Frequently asked questions

How do I choose the right color?

We offer a free color-match service — simply send us a photo taken in natural daylight and our stylists will recommend your perfect shade. Not sure yet? Order $2 color swatches and compare at home before committing to a full set.

Will extensions damage my hair?

Our clip-in extensions require zero glue or heat to apply, making them the gentlest option available. All wefts are silicone-free and crafted from 100% Remy human hair, so they sit naturally and put no stress on your own strands.

How many grams do I need?

As a general guide: fine hair suits 120–160 g, average density hair 160–220 g, and thick or layered hair 220 g or more. Our color-match team can give you a personalised recommendation based on your current hair weight.

How long does shipping take?

Orders placed before 12 pm PST ship the same day. US and Canadian customers receive next-day delivery, with no duties or customs fees on either destination. International orders ship within 1–2 business days.

What's your return policy?

We make returns and exchanges easy. Unopened products in their original packaging are eligible for a full refund or exchange within 30 days of delivery. Simply contact our team and we'll send a prepaid label right away.
