## What they sell
Bold, **queer-owned artisan candles** — hand-poured, non-toxic, plant-based, wooden wicks,
8oz colored glass jars. ~31 SKUs, **$25** core (sets/duets $35–$65). Evocative/queer/mystical
scent names: Cashmere Noir, High Femme, Thistle Spell, Cleopatra's Sails, Spanish Boots, Negroni,
Friend of the Devil, Live Laugh Lesbian, Ma Vie en Rose. Collections: **Pride · Musk & Smoke ·
Gourmand & Spice · Citrus · Woodsy · Seaside**.

  small-batch artisan. "Page of Wands" = tarot (wands = fire/passion/creativity).
- Tone: bold, witty, inclusive, mystical, warm.
