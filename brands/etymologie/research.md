## ICP
Skin-conscious, educated, affluent women ~28–50 (skews 30–45) who are **skeptical of beauty marketing** and want **clinically-proven efficacy AND sustainability**. Concerns: sensitive / compromised barrier, dryness, acne-prone. Values: transparency, science, eco-responsibility, Canadian-made, founder authenticity. Mid-premium → premium price points ($76–$320).
