Our Story

Handmade in Québec, with a little wanderlust.

Caprice & Co began in 2017 in a small Ayer's Cliff atelier, where founder Ketsia Curvers started blending scents that felt like a getaway.

Every candle, oil and perfume is still made by hand, in small batches — vegan, cruelty-free, and a little obsessed with making everyday feel like vacation.
