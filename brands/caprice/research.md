## Brand
**Caprice & Co** — vacation-inspired **body care + fragrance**, handmade in **Ayer's Cliff, QC** (founded 2017, Ketsia
Curvers). Roll-on eau de parfum, dry shimmering body oils, body glow serums, whipped body butters, foaming scrubs,
coconut-wax candles, lip scrubs/masks, linen sprays, bath bombs, bundles. **Destination-named scents:** Capri · Georgia ·
Granada · Hawaii · Monaco · St. Barts. Vegan & cruelty-free, clean fragrance. "Smell like paradise. Every single day."

## ICP
Women 18–35, social, gift-oriented; fun "summery tropical-girl" aesthetic ("Sephora meets beach vacation"). Affordable
($3.99 lip scrub → $64 bundle), gift + self-indulgence. Buy on: scent escapism + fun + clean + giftability.

## USP / value prop
- **Escape/destination fantasy** — each product a vacation: "Inspired by dreamy destinations."
- **Handmade in Canada, small batch** — Ayer's Cliff, QC atelier; "made by hand."
- **Vegan · cruelty-free · clean** — GMO/phthalate/SLS/petroleum-free, IFRA-compliant.
- **Layerable body-care system** — scrub → oil → butter → perfume; "soft, hydrated, scented all day."
- **Widely carried** — "carried in hundreds of stores across North America." Free shipping + free gift over $75.

## Tone
Playful, dreamy, warm, sensory. Vacation daydream, fun femininity (not clinical). Short breezy sentences, ✨; evocative
names ("Tropic Like it's Hot", "Vacay Vibe"). Warm + cheerful + approachable.

## Design tokens (from live site — faithful, then elevated)
- **Real fonts:** **Oranienbaum** (classical thin elegant serif, headings, 400) + **Figtree** (geometric humanist sans, body, 300/400). Both Google Fonts.
  → display Oranienbaum serif + body Figtree.
- **Real palette:** PRIMARY accent **dusty rose #DB5C7B** (their everything — text/headings/links/buttons); page bg baby
  pink **#FFDADA**; surface cream **#FFF7F1**; white; hover dark rose **#D23259**; lighter **#E0718C**; ghost btn **#FFC1C1**.
  → Elevated: warm blush-cream bg + cream surfaces + **rose #DB5C7B** as accent/CTA + a warm tropical **coral/sun** secondary
  + deep plum ink (legible — NOT pink body text). Playful, dreamy, tropical, warm-feminine, premium.
- **Buttons:** radius ~4–6px (near-square), sentence case, rose bg/white text, generous padding (14px 40px). No uppercase.
- **Type:** h1 40px Oranienbaum 400 (no uppercase, no tracking); body 16px Figtree 300, lh 24px. Mixed case.
- **Shape:** buttons near-square 4–6px; CARDS rounded **20px**; minimal shadow; very wide container (full-bleed feel). Generous airy ~60px spacing.
