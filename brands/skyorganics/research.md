# Sky Organics — research

**URL:** https://skyorganics.com/ · **Page:** home · **Language:** EN · **Niche:** skincare-cosmetics (organic/natural beauty)

## Brand
- Sky Organics — clean/organic beauty & body-care. **B Corp certified**, **USDA Organic**, cruelty-free, vegan.
  Single-ingredient, minimal-formula, radical transparency. Founder-led (Steven & Sara Jade) — built after
  becoming parents, frustrated by confusing "natural" claims. One tree planted per order (Ecologi).
  Press: Vogue, InStyle, Byrdie, BuzzFeed.
- **ICP:** women ~25–45, clean-beauty + values-driven, want simple effective organic products that actually work,
  accessible-premium (not gatekept). Parents seeking safer family options.
- **Bestsellers (real, from /collections/best-sellers):** Batana Oil · Organic Castor Oil · Organic Rosemary
  Strong Roots Oil · Organic White Beeswax · Petroleum-free Jelly · Cocoa Butter · Vitamin E Oil.
- **USP:** certified-organic, single-ingredient botanicals with radical transparency — clean care that works,
  affordable to everyone, kinder to the planet.
- **Pains:** greenwashing / fake "natural" claims; confusing ingredient lists & sourcing; guilt over beauty harming planet.
- **Desires:** simple transparent certified-organic that works; align beauty with values; premium that's affordable.
- **Tone:** down-to-earth, purpose-driven, transparent, approachable, warm, unpretentious.

## Look (their brand, elevated — warm-natural organic)
The live site is white + a temporary lavender campaign accent (#c499fb); the ENDURING brand identity is
warm/earthy/organic (golden Glow imagery, amber oils, beeswax, botanicals). Build warm-natural:
- **Palette:** cream/bone bg, deep botanical green ink + buttons, **honey-amber pop** (ties to castor oil/honey/beeswax),
  sage-sand alt fills, soft terracotta for sale/badges. Lots of warm negative space.
- **Type (match their soft geometric heading "bilo"):** display = **Quicksand** (rounded geometric, soft, friendly,
  organic) ; body = **Mulish**. Sentence-case headings, rounded buttons (~pill) — differs from RCMA's condensed uppercase.
- Structure from skincare-cosmetics ref (OSEA DNA = warm-natural — fits directly this time).
