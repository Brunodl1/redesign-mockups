the craft

hand-poured, the slow way

coconut wax burns cleaner and longer than paraffin, holding scent without the smoke. we fragrance each candle only with RIFM-certified essential oils, set a single clean wick, and pour by hand in small batches in ojai, california — slow enough to get every one right.

  • clean coconut wax recyclable, with a longer, cleaner burn
  • essential-oil scent RIFM-certified, no dyes or phthalates
  • hand-poured in ojai small batches, made by hand

our story

made in ojai, for everyone

fond. was born in ojai, california from a simple belief — that scent should transcend gender. no rules, no labels, just light, warmth and the quiet ritual of a flame.

every candle is hand-poured with recyclable coconut wax and pure essential oils, then packed in minimal, eco-friendly materials made to be kept, refilled or returned to the earth.
