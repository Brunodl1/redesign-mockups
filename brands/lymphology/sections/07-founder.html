OUR STORY

Lymphology was founded by Maddie Simone, a Certified Lymphedema Therapist, after years of helping patients manage swelling, circulation, and skin health. She built the cream she wished existed — clinically sound, clean, and made in Canada.

Today it's Canada's only skincare line focused on lymphatic care — trusted by therapists, patients, and anyone who wants skin that feels lighter and healthier.
