## ICP
Women 30–60 with lymphedema/lipedema/venous insufficiency/post-surgical edema/circulation issues (often in compression) +
general wellness women 25–55 into de-puffing/circulation/clean body care. Gender-neutral lean. Accessible mid-market ($25–$137 CAD).
Buy on: clinical credibility + de-puffing/circulation results + clean/natural + Canadian-made.
