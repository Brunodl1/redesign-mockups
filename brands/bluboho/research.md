# Research — bluboho (bluboho.com)

**Page:** home · **Language:** EN (English-only Canadian brand — no FR storefront/toggle; "jewellery" CA spelling, CAD pricing, ON/AB/BC stores) · **Niche:** jewelry (reference: `references/jewelry.md`, exemplar Mejuri — warm it toward bluboho's earthy boho-luxe) · **Slug:** bluboho

## Brand
- **What they sell:** fine/refined handcrafted jewellery — rings, necklaces, earrings, bracelets, charms & chains. 10K+ gold, real gemstones (emerald, sapphire, opal, lapis, larimar, amethyst, jasper). Price ~$398–$998 CAD. Signature collections: **Healing Garden** (botanical charms, opal necklaces — current hero campaign, "enter the garden"), **Engagement Rings / bluboho Brides**, **Mood Ring** (zodiac/birthstone), **Beloved by bluboho**. Hero pieces: Passion Flower Charm ($998), Green Opal Bloom Beaded Necklace ($798), Forget-Me-Not Flower Charm ($548), Emerald Beaded Mood Bracelet ($698).
- **ICP:** women 25–45 buying jewellery as meaningful markers of life moments (engagement, birthday, breakup, new beginning, self-milestone); value symbolic meaning + story over trend; drawn to Canadian-made accessible-luxury; appreciate brand narrative + healing/wellness language; intentional gift-givers.
- **USP (real copy):** "Tell your story with our handcrafted jewellery" · "Raw and refined pieces to mark every moment" · "We believe life's moments are a legacy & we exist to ensure they live on" · handcrafted · **free shipping on Canadian orders over $250** · **1% for the Planet** member · birthstone/zodiac mood collections · **contemplation cards** ("ask a question, set an intention, pull a card to receive a message from the universe") · 6 physical stores (Ottawa, Oakville, Calgary, Toronto ×2, Vancouver).
- **Tone:** poetic, introspective, soulful — jewellery as spiritual/emotional amulet, not accessory. e.g. "born of the restless wind, a gemini moves with a shimmering curiosity"; "after separating from my husband, I wanted something to mark my new beginning."
- **Pains:** gifted/bought jewellery feels generic & impersonal · hard to find pieces that mark meaningful transitions · want ethical/environmental reassurance in a luxury buy.
- **Desires:** own pieces with story/spiritual meaning ("amulets to guide your journey") · wearable reminders of self-identity (birthstone/zodiac) · gifting that feels intentional + carries emotional weight.
- **Logo:** `https://cdn.shopify.com/oxygen-v2/44841/40612/85499/3692651/assets/bluboho-logo-black-BYJW_itZ.png` (transparent black PNG; alt "bluboho logo", lowercase brand). Invert on dark footer.
