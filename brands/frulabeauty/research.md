## Brand
- **Frula Beauty** — **fruit-formulated**, naturally-derived **skincare + haircare + bodycare**. Made in **New Zealand**, dermatologically tested, cruelty-free, for **all skin types incl. sensitive**. Accessible-luxury K-beauty energy ("dewy", "bouncy", "milky") with clinical trust underneath. Hero ingredients: Vitamin C, Hyaluronic Acid, Niacinamide, Ceramide, Retinol, Rosehip.
- Sub-brands on packaging: **FRULASKIN**, **FRULAHAIR** (note in copy hierarchy).
- **ICP:** 20s–40s glow-seekers wanting affordable, natural, ingredient-led skincare that's gentle enough for sensitive skin; like K-beauty dewy results without prestige prices.
- **USP:** fruit-formulated, naturally-derived beauty — dewy, ingredient-led results across skin, hair & body, derm-tested + cruelty-free + NZ-made, gentle on sensitive skin, at accessible prices.
- **Pains:** harsh/drying actives, confusing routines, prestige prices, sensitivity flare-ups.
- **Desires:** dewy bouncy glow, gentle natural formulas that actually work, simple affordable routine, trustworthy sourcing.
- **Tone:** warm, approachable, glow-focused; fruit-natural + clinical trust signals.
