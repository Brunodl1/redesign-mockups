Our Story

Hand-Poured in Pittsburgh Since 2017

Sweet Water Decor began with founder Melissa Horvath and a single candle — a small idea poured by hand in Pittsburgh, PA, with a wish to make everyday moments feel a little brighter.

Today, our woman-owned team hand-pours every candle with a 100% soy wax blend and clean, non-toxic ingredients — made to bring a little warmth and encouragement into your home.
