OUR STORY

40 years of water-wellness science

Malibu C was founded in 1985 on a simple discovery — that hard water, chlorine and oxidation, not your hair, were the real problem. They pioneered crystallized vitamin-C wellness remedies that professional stylists have trusted for four decades.

Today Malibu C is an employee-owned company still making every formula in the USA, vegan and cruelty-free. The science is unchanged. The commitment is deeper than ever.
