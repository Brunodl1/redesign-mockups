## Brand
- Malibu C (Malibu Wellness) — vitamin-C-powered hair & body **wellness remedies**. Founded 1985 (40 yrs),
  employee-owned (ESOP). Signature **single-use crystal packets** ("Crystal Gel Technology"): pure vitamin-C
  ascorbic-acid crystals that activate with tap water to **remove hard-water minerals, chlorine, and oxidative
  buildup** from hair. Vegan, cruelty-free, paraben/sulfate/gluten-free, made in USA. Dual channel: salons + DTC.
- **ICP:** results-driven shoppers with a SPECIFIC problem — hard water (85% of US homes), chlorine/swimmers,
  brassy color, dull/buildup. Want a proven mechanism, not vague "nourishing". Plus pro stylists.
- **USP:** crystallized vitamin C that dissolves the minerals/chlorine wrecking your hair — science, not hype.
- **Pains:** hard-water mineral buildup (dull/brittle); chlorine & pool damage; product/residue + oxidative buildup.
- **Desires:** cleaner/shinier/stronger hair with visible results; simple science-based fix; eco-conscious.
- **Tone:** educational authority, approachable, science-credible (not cold-clinical). "Results You Can C."
