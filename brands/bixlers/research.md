## Brand
Bixlers — **America's Oldest Jewelers, established 1785**. Fine jewelry crafted to celebrate life's milestones (engagement, anniversary, gifts). In-house design collections (Hologlam, Interlinked, Sculpture, Fine Jewelry), bridal/engagement, heritage storefront. Complimentary standard shipping. Elegant, heritage-meets-modern, very light/airy site.

## ICP
Fine-jewelry shoppers (self-purchase + milestone/gift), ~28–65, value craftsmanship + heritage + trust. Buying emotion: "beautiful, special, will last, from a house I can trust (240 years!)." Objection: quality/authenticity/worth-it & trust → answer with heritage (since 1785), craftsmanship/materials, gorgeous imagery, reviews, warranty, appointments, easy returns.

## USP / proof

## Pains → Desires
Pains: trusting an online jeweler, quality/authenticity worry, finding something special. Desires: timeless beauty, craftsmanship, heritage trust, a piece that marks the moment, easy elegant buying.

## Design tokens (REAL brand — match first; KEEP IT LIGHT)
- **Palette:** **white / near-white** base (`#ffffff`, `#FAFAFC`, soft grey `#eceff6`), near-black ink `#1a1d24`, **deep NAVY `#1c2b4a`** accent (top bar / footer / buttons — used sparingly), **gold `#b8893f`** hairline accent; gold + gemstone color comes from product photos. Airy, generous whitespace.
- **Type:** light, elegant — **Jost** (light/elegant geometric sans) for headings + body (light weights, generous letter-spacing). Real **script "Bixler's 1785" logo** used as-is.
- **Buttons:** navy filled primary + ink-outline secondary; small radius (4px), uppercase letter-spacing; minimal. Lots of air.
