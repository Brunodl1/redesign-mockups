# RCMA Makeup — research

**URL:** https://rcmamakeup.com/ · **Page:** home · **Language:** EN · **Niche:** skincare-cosmetics (pro makeup)

## Brand
- **RCMA** = Research Council of Makeup Artists. 60+ yr legacy; founded on Vincent Kehoe's
  theatrical/film makeup research. The pro's pro brand.
- **ICP:** Professional makeup artists (film/theatre/editorial/bridal) + serious enthusiasts
  who want artist-grade product. Credibility-driven, not trend-driven.
- **Hero products:** No Color Powder (translucent setting powder, the icon) · VK 18-Part
  Foundation/Concealer Palettes · Highlight & Contour Palettes · Premiere/Loose Powders.
- **USP:** Made by working makeup artists, for makeup artists. Research-backed, set-tested
  formulas trusted on professional sets for decades.
- **Pains:** consumer powders break down under lights/long days; foundations don't shade-match
  diverse skin; few brands understand pro workflow.
- **Desires:** formulas that perform under heat/lights/long shoots; deep shade range for
  precise matching; tools built by people who actually do the job.
- **Tone:** professional, understated, functional. Credibility over hype. No fluff.

## Look (pull from THE BRAND, not the warm-natural reference)
- **Palette:** strict black/white/warm-gray pro aesthetic. Charcoal ink, bone/cream bg,
  white cards. Introduce ONE ownable accent (brand has none): warm **clay/nude** (#B0714F)
  tying to foundation/skin tones — used sparingly for eyebrow/badges/rule.
- **Fonts (their real ones):** Archivo Narrow (condensed display) + IBM Plex Sans (body). Both on Google Fonts.
- **Buttons:** rectangular, minimal, square corners, uppercase — utilitarian pro feel.
