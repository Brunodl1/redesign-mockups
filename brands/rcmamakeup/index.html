The RCMA Story

Born in a makeup artist's research lab.

RCMA — the Research Council of Makeup Artists — was founded by Vincent Kehoe, a makeup authority and educator who didn't just teach the craft: he researched and built the formulas his students needed. Film, television, theatrical artists. Professionals who couldn't afford failure under lights.

Six decades later, RCMA remains the kit staple passed down on sets. The No Color Powder and foundation palettes endure not because of marketing, but because working artists keep putting them back in the case.
