Our Story

Made by hand, for skin that needs more.

My Emollient started in a Brooklyn kitchen — a search for something that actually worked for a family member's eczema when everything else failed. Synthetic fillers, water-stretched lotions, overpromising creams: none of it helped. So we made our own.

Every jar is still handmade in small batches with just 7 certified organic ingredients. No water, no fillers — just rich plant butters that go to work the moment they touch your skin. That's the whole idea.
