## Brand
- Bariatric Fusion — **single-brand** maker of specialized supplements for **weight-loss-surgery (bariatric)
  patients** (gastric bypass, sleeve, band, duodenal switch). **ASMBS-guideline compliant**, formulated by
  **bariatric surgeons + registered dietitians**, **all-in-one chewables** (multivitamin + calcium + more in
  fewer, great-tasting doses → no pill fatigue), wide flavor range, GLP-1 support line. 10+ yrs, surgeon-trusted.
- **ICP:** post-op bariatric patients (esp. 6+ mo) managing deficiency risk + daily compliance; pre-op; caregivers;
  bariatric surgeons/dietitians recommending to patients.
- **USP:** complete, surgeon-formulated bariatric nutrition that meets/exceeds ASMBS guidelines — in fewer,
  great-tasting chewables instead of a handful of pills. "Your success is our success."
- **Pains:** nutrient deficiency/poor absorption post-surgery; pill fatigue / hard-to-swallow tablets;
  complicated, pricey, bad-tasting multi-supplement regimens (low compliance).
- **Desires:** one simple daily routine (fewer doses); supplements that taste good (not medicine); affordable, surgeon-approved, clinically validated.
- **Tone:** supportive, empowering, educational, patient-centric, warm-but-clinical (lifelong wellness partner).
