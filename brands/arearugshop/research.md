# Area Rug Shop — research (home redesign)

## Brand
- **Area Rug Shop** (arearugshop.ca, Shopify `arearugshop.myshopify.com`). **Language: EN** (`/fr` 404; Ontario retailer; "Quebec" = province dropdown, not Québec-based → English build).
- **What:** Canada's largest area-rug retailer. Rugs in all sizes/shapes/textures/materials/colours; accessories, welcome mats, gift cards. 5 showrooms: Barrie, Etobicoke, Kitchener, Toronto, Vaughan. Free design consultation, trade program.
- **USP:** selection + scale ("Canada's largest"), in-person showrooms + online, free consultation, premium collections (Kendal-Iris, Harlowe & Willow, Premium). Big-ticket considered purchase.
- **ICP:** homeowners/renters 30–60 furnishing living/dining/bedroom + interior designers (trade). Objections: right size/material for my room? quality worth it? safe to buy a big rug online? → shop-by-space, materials story, showrooms/heritage, free consult, returns.
- **Niche:** rugs (reference NEW — exemplar **The Rug Company**, approved 2026-06-11; reinterpreted dark+gold).
