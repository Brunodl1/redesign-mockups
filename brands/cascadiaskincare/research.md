# Cascadia Skincare — research (home redesign)

- **URL:** https://cascadiaskincare.com/  ·  **Brand:** Cascadia Skincare
- **Language:** **EN** (Vancouver, BC; .com; English-first)
- **Niche:** skincare-cosmetics (natural / botanical / handmade — heritage since 1990)
- **Page:** home  ·  **Hero variant:** full-bleed lush-forest lifestyle (their signature) — elevated

## ICP / positioning
Values-driven women ~30–60 who want **natural, holistic, handmade** skincare from a real,
long-standing maker — and feel good about ingredients + origin. Buying emotion: "honest,
small-batch, nature-made remedies from a brand that's done this since 1990." Objection:
*"is 'natural' effective / is this just crunchy?"* → answer with heritage (30+ yrs), real
botanicals, holistic remedies, reviews, made-in-Canada small-batch craft.

## USP / proof
- **"Natural Wellness, Made in Vancouver."** Handmade, holistic skin remedies + soaps,
  small-batch in BC, Canada. **Crafted since 1990** (heritage = key differentiator).
  "Hand-made with love & ingredients you can believe in."
- Range: cleansers/soap bars, serums, salves, body creams, hair bars. Botanical, gentle.
