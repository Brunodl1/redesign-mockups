- **Language:** **EN** (Toronto/Ontario brand; .ca + FR toggle exist but Bruno confirmed English)
- **Niche:** skincare-cosmetics (clean / minimalist beauty)
- **Page:** home  ·  **Hero variant:** full-bleed lifestyle (elevating their real hero) — Strategy & Seaflora were full-bleed/split, this is a *well-executed* full-bleed with a contained panel

## ICP / positioning
Skin-conscious women ~25–50 who want **clean, gentle, no-nonsense skincare** and a
self-care ritual. Buying emotion: "honest, minimalist, non-toxic skincare that reveals my
natural glow — no filters, no fillers." Objection: *"is 'clean' just marketing?"* → answer
with the clean-formulation promise (cruelty-free, fragrance-free, non-toxic, vegan),
5.0★ reviews, Made-in-Canada, real product transparency.

## USP / proof
- **"Glow naturally — skincare that puts you first."** / "Reveal your skin's natural power."
