Tea 101

Brew it right.

Simple rituals, remarkable cups. Three steps are all it takes.

  1. 01

    Heat

    Match the water to the leaf — 175°F for green, boiling for black & pu'er.

  2. 02

    Steep

    1 tsp per cup, 1–3 minutes. Taste as you go.

  3. 03

    Re-steep

    Whole leaf gives 3+ infusions — each one a little different.
