Our story

100% Indigenous-owned, proudly

Tribalure is a 100% Indigenous-owned Canadian beauty company. Every shade, every product and every name carries heritage, story and pride — bold colour made with care and meaning.

We created Tribalure to bring real representation to the beauty aisle and to give back to community. This is our roots, our fire and our smoke — honoured in every formula and shared with you.
