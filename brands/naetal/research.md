## Brand
**Naetal Skincare** — small-batch mama-&-baby skincare made in **BC, Canada**, for the full motherhood journey (prenatal →
postpartum → baby). Founder **Vivian**, who created it after a severe pregnancy rash (PUPPPs). Clean, simple (8–10
ingredients), **pregnancy-safe & nursing-safe**, non-toxic. Dual-use (same formulas for mom's skin + baby's). Warm,
nurturing, community-forward (NICU/"Mom Talk" stories, "Mamas for Mamas" giving-back). Tagline "Simple & Clean Skin Care for
Moms and Babies." Hero copy "calm and soothe delicate skin." **Postpartum sweet spot:** Belly Butter, Postpartum Mama Herbal Bath, Original Serum (healing/stretch marks/PUPPPs).

## ICP
New & expecting mothers ~25–38; concerns: pregnancy rash (PUPPPs), stretch marks, postpartum healing (C-section scar,
sensitivity, hyperpigmentation), dryness, baby eczema; ingredient-safety anxiety. Strong **gifting** (baby showers, new-mom
gifts). Mid-market accessible-premium (CAD: $10 lip → singles $22–$88 → bundles $60–$135).

## USP / value prop
- **Simple & clean for moms & babies** — non-toxic, no fillers; pregnancy- & nursing-safe.
- **Made for the 4th trimester** — postpartum healing, stretch marks, herbal mama bath; + prenatal & baby.
- **Essential ingredients** — only 8–10 per product, each with a purpose.
- **Dual-use** — same formula soothes mom's rash and baby's eczema.
- **Small-batch, made in Canada (BC)** · founder-made (Vivian's own pregnancy-rash story).

## Tone
Warm, nurturing, matter-of-fact clean-beauty — a friend who's been through it. Not clinical, not glam. Empathy + community
(real mom stories). Gentle, soft, modern. No fear-mongering, no jargon.

## Design tokens (from live site — faithful, then elevated)
- **Real fonts:** **Crimson Text** (elegant oldstyle serif — headings/logo) + **Nunito** (rounded warm sans — body). Both Google Fonts. Ideal maternity pairing.
- **Real palette:** PRIMARY accent **clay-rose #AE877B** (CTAs, footer); page bg **#E6DDD9** (pinkish greige); ink **#5B4C47**
  (warm brown); surfaces #EFEDEA / #DCCFCA; border #E1D8CE (warm sand); nav/drawer #89695F (clay brown). → Elevated: soft warm
  greige bg + warm off-white cards + clay-rose accent + deep warm-brown footer + a soft sage for botanical/clean cues (sparing). Tender, nurturing, premium.
- **Buttons:** real = SQUARE clay (radius 0), uppercase tracked .1em. → elevate to **rounded/pill** clay (gentle, nurturing); keep uppercase tracked.
- **Type:** Crimson serif headings (mixed case, 28px, wt400 light), Nunito body 15px lh~1.7; eyebrows/labels uppercase tracked. Soft.
- **Shape:** real = square (radius 0). → soften to **rounded** (cards ~14px, images ~22px, pill buttons) for tender maternity warmth. Container ~1180px. Generous 80px section spacing.
