Good to Know

Questions, answered.

How much calcium is in each chew?

Each chew delivers 500 mg of dietary calcium, alongside vitamin D, K2 (MK-7), magnesium, and phosphorus — a complete cast of nutrients that work together to support bone density.

Is it really made from real food?

Yes — the calcium in Seen comes from whole-food sources, not synthetic calcium carbonate. Just 5 simple, recognisable ingredients. Nothing synthetic, nothing unnecessary.

Does it have added sugar?

No added sugar — not a gram. It's naturally delicious, so you'll actually look forward to taking it every day.

Is it safe if I'm lactose intolerant?

Absolutely. Seen is completely dairy-free, making it a great calcium option for anyone with a sensitive stomach or lactose intolerance.

How many do I take per day?

Most women enjoy one to two chews daily — one with breakfast, one with dinner works well. As always, follow the guidance of your healthcare provider for your individual needs.

Is it third-party tested?

Yes. Every batch is independently tested for microbes, heavy metals, and nutrient levels. You can trust exactly what's on the label.

Can it help with osteoporosis?

Seen is formulated to support daily bone health and is recommended by osteoporosis specialists as part of a healthy routine. It is not a medical treatment — if you have a diagnosis, please speak with your doctor about the right approach for you.
