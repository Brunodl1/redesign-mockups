Our story

Made by hand in Squamish, BC

bonosensu began as one maker, Kalisa, turning a quiet love of Scandinavian-inspired design and slow handcraft into small-batch home goods made right here in Squamish, British Columbia.

Every clay ornament is shaped, stamped and finished by hand — so no two are ever exactly alike. Small marks and gentle variations aren't flaws; they're the fingerprints of something truly made by a person, for your home.

Warmly, Kalisa
