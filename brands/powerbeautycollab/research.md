# Power Beauty Collab (PBC) — research + tokens + image plan

## Brand
- **Name:** Power Beauty Collab (PBC) — a curated **multi-brand MARKETPLACE** for
  clean indie beauty & wellness. US. Page: home. Language: **EN**.
- **Sells (curates):** Fragrances, Haircare, Skincare (Cleansers/Moisturizers/
  Serums/Toners/Masks), Sunscreen, Bath & Body, Cosmetics, Beauty Tools, Travel
  Sizes, Men's — products from many indie brands (Veriphy, BimeBeauty, Sumbody,
  Vaihea, Topical Skin…).
- **USP / mission:** "THE place to shop clean indie beauty & wellness brands."
  "The Power of Beauty is Yours." Clean formulas — no sulfates, parabens,
  phthalates. **PBC Certification** (their clean standard). Health-education angle
  (88 toxins across 73,000+ products; phthalates/formaldehyde concerns).
- **ICP:** conscious clean-beauty shoppers ~25–45, sustainability-minded, wary of
  harmful cosmetic chemicals. Objection the page must answer: "is 'clean' real, and
  can I trust this curation?" → answer with the **PBC Certification / clean
  standard**, free-from claims, brand vetting, reviews.
- **Tone:** informative, empowering, mission-driven, approachable.
- **Niche:** clean-beauty-marketplace → reference `references/clean-beauty-marketplace.md`
  (Credo Beauty DNA), approved by Bruno 2026-06-15.
