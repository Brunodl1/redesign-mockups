The Science

vitamin c neutralizes chlorine

neutralizes chlorine on contact — in seconds.

Chlorine oxidizes and clings to hair and skin, causing dryness, brittleness and that green tint. Ascorbic acid (Vitamin C) chemically reduces chlorine into harmless, water-soluble compounds — so it rinses clean away instead of staying in.
