## Brand
Honey Sparkle — **Montréal beauty & wellness brand**: hand-poured **soy candles**, **skincare**, **body care**, plus an intimate-care/aftercare line. Tagline: "Beauté & bien-être en harmonie / Where beauty meets wellness." Positioning: **affordable luxury, indulgent self-care, glow every day**, for all skin types. Free shipping on Canadian orders over $99.

## ICP
Women ~20–40, self-care & "treat yourself" shoppers who want affordable little luxuries (a $30 candle, a $25 glow serum, body butter). Buying emotion: "pamper myself, glow, cozy ritual, feel good — without breaking the bank." Objection to answer: *"is it actually nice / worth it?"* → answer with warmth, reviews, glow/results, bundles, gifting, free shipping.

## USP / proof
- Beauty + wellness in one warm brand: candles + skincare + body care.
- Affordable luxury (candles $30, serum $24.99, body butter $27.99).
- Hand-poured soy candles; skincare for all skin types; Montréal-made self-care.
- Free shipping over $99 (Canada); gifting / discovery sets.

## Pains → Desires
Pains: self-care feels expensive/indulgent-guilty; generic products; want a cozy ritual. Desires: glow, cozy ambiance, affordable treat, a brand that feels warm & personal, gift-ready.
