Our Story

Born from a gap in the mirror.

I grew up never seeing my skin tone reflected on a beauty shelf — blushes turned ashy, 'nudes' were never mine. So I built the brand I wished I'd had.

Soniaz celebrates South-Asian beauty — our culture, our skin, our shades — authentically and without clichés. Proudly South-Asian owned, Halal-certified, and vegan.
