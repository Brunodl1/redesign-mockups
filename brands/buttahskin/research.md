## Brand
- Buttah Skin — Black-owned skincare founded by **Dorion Renaud** (Beaumont, TX), **shea-butter** core
  + hi-tech actives, formulated for **melanin-rich skin** (and all tones). Targets **dark spots /
  hyperpigmentation / uneven tone**, sunscreen with **no white cast**. 6,000+ 5-star reviews,
  dermatologist Dr. Rawn Bosley, subscription + Buttah Fam community.
- **ICP:** women with melanin-rich skin (25–50) fighting dark spots/uneven tone, wanting luxe-yet-
  effective skincare from a brand that understands deeper complexions.
- **Hero products:** Anti-Dark Spot 15% Vitamin C Serum · Everlasting Retinol Oil · Invisible Tinted
  Mineral Sunscreen SPF 30 · Facial Shea Butter / CocoShea Cream · Dark Spot Defense Kit.
- **USP:** skincare engineered for melanin-rich skin — fades dark spots, evens tone, no white cast — by a founder who shares the experience.
- **Pains:** hyperpigmentation/dark spots; sunscreen white cast/residue; few brands understand deep complexions.
- **Desires:** radiant even skin celebrating melanin; luxe clinically-proven products; community + a founder who gets it.
- **Tone:** celebratory, inclusive, empowering, expert, luxe-accessible.

## Look — pull from BUTTAH, structure from skincare-cosmetics ref (warm-natural)
- **Palette:** warm cream #FFF8F1 base + gold #D4BA88 accent + **charcoal #262624** ink + charcoal CTAs (cream text).
  Deepen the gold for TEXT/eyebrows/stars (#D4BA88 fails AA as text → use ~#8C6A26).
- **Fonts:** body/UI = **Montserrat** (their real font; UPPERCASE buttons, 0.2em tracking, ~3px radius);
  display = warm serif (**Fraunces**) for big editorial headlines (elevates the melanin-celebrating warmth).
- **Header cream** (black+gold logo reads); **footer charcoal** → logo on a cream chip (black part would vanish on charcoal).
