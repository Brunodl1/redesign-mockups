Our Story

Made by us,
for our skin.

Buttah was born from a feeling Dorion Renaud knew too well — shelves full of skincare that simply wasn't built for us. Growing up in Beaumont, Texas, he watched melanin-rich skin get overlooked, under-served, and handed products that left white casts and faded in the morning light. So he went back to something older: shea butter, a ritual passed down through generations, and a trust that runs deeper than any trend.

He blended that heritage with modern actives — ingredients that work with our skin to fade dark spots, even tone, and let your natural glow lead. No compromise, no afterthought. Today, Buttah isn't just a skincare line; it's the Buttah Fam — a community of people who finally have products that see them, celebrate them, and show up every day for them.

— Dorion Renaud, Founder
