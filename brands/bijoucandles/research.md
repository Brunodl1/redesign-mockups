# Bijou Candles — research

**URL:** https://bijoucandles.com/ · **Page:** home · **Language:** EN · **Niche:** candles (pop-culture/witchy)

## Brand
- Bijou Candles — **pop-culture / character-inspired** luxury **soy candles** (+ apparel). Each candle is
  named after / inspired by beloved cult films & TV (Practical Magic → Gillian/Sally, Mamma Mia → Donna/
  Tanya/Rosie, The Love Witch → Elaine, etc). Hand-poured soy, made in USA, room-filling scent.
  "Icon-level fragrance without the celebrity price." NOT a jewelry candle (verified). 4,600+ reviews;
  press: BuzzFeed, Cosmopolitan, Teen Vogue, Allure. Witchy/nostalgic/irreverent tone.
- **ICP:** nostalgic millennials & Gen Z (25–45, female-leaning) who love cult films/TV + want identity-
  driven self-care & gifts; witchy/coven/nostalgia coded; gift-givers.
- **Candles (real, cdn.shopify canonical):** Lena (Lavender & Chamomile) · Greta (Bourbon & Vanilla) ·
  Tanya (Aegean Lemon Zest) · Gillian (Book of Shadows) · Donna (Sea Salt & Pink Sand) · Sally · Rosie ·
  Elaine (Enchanted Amber, tall $48) · sets: Practical Magic ($88), Dynamos/Mamma Mia ($129). Candles ~$44.
- **USP:** premium room-filling soy candles named after the films & characters that shaped you — luxe scent + nostalgia, at half the celebrity-candle price.
- **Pains:** luxury scent brands cost too much; hard to find tasteful fandom/nostalgia goods; generic home fragrance is boring/anonymous.
- **Desires:** room-filling premium scent tied to beloved pop culture; wearable/displayable identity; quality (soy, USA, hand-poured) without the markup.
- **Tone:** irreverent, witchy/mystical, nostalgic, culturally-aware, luxe-accessible.

## Look — elevate their minimal black/white into WITCHY-EDITORIAL (candles ref / Boy Smells DNA)
Their real site is timid default-Shopify white+black+UPPERCASE-dark-buttons — the candles ref says AVOID that.
Elevate: warm **parchment cream** canvas + deep **plum-black** ink/bands + **GOLD accent** (ties to "Bijou" =
jewel) + let the colorful candle jars punch. Keep their dark UPPERCASE buttons.
- **Palette:** cream #F5EEE0 bg, deep plum-black ink #271826, gold pop #9A6F22 (eyebrows/accents, deeper for AA),
  brighter gold stars #C8902F, witchy-berry secondary #9C3B63, dark plum footer.
- **Fonts:** display **Cormorant Garamond** (elegant high-contrast witchy-luxe serif, 600/700) + body **Inter**.
  Buttons UPPERCASE tracked (their style), dark fill.
- Structure from candles ref: hero → trust → shop-by-scent-family tiles → bestseller grid (scent notes+price+★) →
  pop-culture COLLECTIONS band (the differentiator) → scent-story/how-made dark band → reviews+press → gift/newsletter → footer.
