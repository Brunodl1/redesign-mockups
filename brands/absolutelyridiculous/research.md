## Brand / ICP
- **What they sell:** bold, design-forward baseball/softball gear — custom **fielding gloves**,
  **sliding mitts** (the hero category, "world's fastest"), **batting gloves**, **protective gear**,
  apparel. Loud colorways + athlete collabs (Elly De La Cruz, Jazz Chisholm) + named drops (Miami Ice,
  Ice Cream, "Trust the Ridiculous").
- **ICP:** athletes youth→pro + parents buying for kids + fans buying the drop. Buying emotion:
  identity/status/self-expression + "my kid looks unstoppable." Objection: "worth the premium & is it
  good?" → answered by 8,192 reviews, pro endorsement, colorways.
- **USP:** "Absolutely Ridiculous innovation for Athletes" · "functional art" · speed/innovation
  (world's fastest sliding mitt) · unique aesthetics + craftsmanship + collabs.
- **Tone:** playful, confident, youthful, lowercase. Big social proof (**8,192 reviews**).
- **Announcement idea:** "free shipping over $75 · 8,192 five-star reviews".

## Design tokens (REAL — read from rendered screenshot)
- **Dark cinematic:** near-black **#0A0A0A** canvas, white **#FFFFFF** type, **electric-orange
  #FF6719** signature accent (+ crossed-bats "X" mark), royal-blue **#2F6BFF** secondary, grey
  #A8A8A8 labels. UI is moody/premium; **color comes from the loud product photography**, not chrome.
- Type: **rounded playful LOWERCASE display** (brand wordmark + headlines) → **Fredoka**; body
  **Inter**; eyebrow labels UPPERCASE letter-spaced.
- Buttons: **pill**, lowercase, "shop … →" with arrow; hero CTA = ghost (white outline) on imagery;
  solid CTA = orange fill.
- Logo: white wordmark `assets/logo.png` (transparent, light → visible on dark). Orange "X" mark
  `assets/xmark.png` as accent glyph. Footer uses same white logo (already light).
- Mood: dark editorial storefront; products explode with color; playful lowercase type; orange + X signature.
- Aligns with [[dark-cinematic-standard]]: type owns canvas, accent≠fill, mono labels, unified grade + grain.
