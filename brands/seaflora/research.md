## ICP / positioning
Skin-conscious, values-driven women ~30–60 who want **clean, natural, proven** skincare
and care about sustainability/origin. Buying emotion: "the ocean's purest ingredients,
hand-made by a real BC family, and actually award-winning — not greenwashing."
Objection to answer: *"does 'natural seaweed' really work?"* → answer with the **35+ awards**,
spa trust, ingredient science (bioavailable marine nutrients), before/after, reviews.

## USP / proof
- **"The Ocean, Bottled."** The only skincare brand made with **whole, raw, wild seaweed** —
  not water, not extracts. **Hand-harvested from the Salish Sea**, food-grade formulations,
  trusted by leading spas, **backed by 35+ awards**. Made in Sooke, BC, Canada.
- Benefits copy (real): rich in bioavailable nutrients → improves elasticity, smooths fine
  lines, enhances natural glow; seaweed calms visible redness & supports the skin barrier;
  deep moisture & replenishment.
- Tagline alt: "Nature's Seacret to Radiant Skin."
