Questions, Answered

Is it suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes. Eternal Youth is formulated clean and waterless — no fillers, fragrance, or unnecessary additives. The concentrate contains only biocompatible actives your skin recognises. As with any new skincare, a brief patch test is advised before first use.

When will I see results?

Most users notice calmer, more radiant skin within 1–2 weeks of consistent use. Visible improvements in firmness and plumping typically emerge between weeks 7 and 12 as deeper cellular repair takes effect.

Can it replace my serums?

Yes — completely. Twenty-five concentrated actives are precision-blended into a single step. There is no need to layer multiple serums; Eternal Youth replaces your entire multi-serum routine without compromise.

How long does a bottle last?

Used twice daily with 3–4 drops each application: the 15 ml bottle lasts approximately 6–8 weeks; the 40 ml bottle lasts 4–5 months. Because a little goes a long way, resist the temptation to over-apply.

Is it cruelty-free and clean?

Always. Purana is permanently committed to cruelty-free practices and clean actives — no synthetic fillers, no animal testing, ever. Every order ships carbon-neutral at no extra cost to you.
